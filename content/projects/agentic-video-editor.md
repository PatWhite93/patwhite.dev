An agentic system that processes raw skateboard session footage and uses LLM-driven analysis to identify successful trick attempts, automatically cutting them into highlight reels.

Combines computer vision for scene detection with LLM reasoning for trick identification and quality assessment.

Built because I got tired of scrubbing through hours of footage to find the 30 seconds of actual tricks.
